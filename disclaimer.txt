

This tracker is a visualization tool for monitoring disease transmission chains and is NOT an official public health resource.
DATA SOURCES & LIMITATIONS

    Case data is manually entered from public sources (news reports, WHO briefings, official announcements)
    Data may be incomplete, outdated, or contain errors from source reporting
    Case status (suspected/confirmed) reflects information available at time of entry and may change
    Laboratory-confirmed vs suspected cases are based on publicly reported information only
    This tracker is NOT connected to official health authority databases or real-time reporting systems

TRANSMISSION CHAIN ACCURACY

    Transmission connections are based on available contact tracing data from public sources
    Person-to-person transmission is inferred from reported exposure, not epidemiological investigation
    Exposure timelines, flight manifests, and cabin assignments may be incomplete or approximate
    Secondary transmission risk calculations are estimates only, not epidemiological predictions

OFFICIAL INFORMATION SOURCES

    WHO Emergency Response: who.int/emergencies
    CDC Outbreak Tracking: cdc.gov
    ECDC (Europe): ecdc.europa.eu
    Local public health authorities in your region

INTENDED USE

Designed for personal research, situational awareness, and understanding transmission patterns.
⛔ DO NOT USE FOR: official contact tracing  ·  public health decision-making  ·  travel or quarantine decisions  ·  medical advice
ACCURACY RESPONSIBILITY

Data entry and visualization are subject to human error. Always verify critical information with official health sources before making any decisions based on this tracker.
